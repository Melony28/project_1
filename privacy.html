PRIVACY POLICY

Mane are committed to ensuring the privacy of your information. We understand that as a customer using the Mane online website how important the privacy of your personal information is to you. The following statement addresses relevant privacy issues for Mane. However, if you have any further questions relating to this privacy statement, please do not hesitate to email us at: sales@Mane.net.au When You Provide Information When you provide us with your personal details ie. When joining our mailing list or purchasing items, we may request personal information such as your name and address and your e-mail address. This information is fundamental in order for you to carry on business with us, and so that we may contact you when necessary on issues relating directly to the product or service. This practice is consistent with our normal business policies. We will not use your personal information for any purpose which is not related to the products or services we provide to you or for any purpose for which you would not reasonably expect us to use the information. It is your decision whether you wish us to provide this service to you. We will not sell or disclose your information to any individual or entity for marketing purposes. Security of Captured Data All stored customer information is protected from unauthorised access through the use of secure passwords and user logons or other security procedures. Storage of Collected Information The security of your personal information is important to us. When you enter sensitive information (such as credit card numbers) on our website, we encrypt that information using secure socket layer technology (SSL). When Credit Card details are collected, we simply pass them on in order to be processed as required. We never permanently store complete Credit Card details. We follow generally accepted industry standards to protect the personal information submitted to us, both during transmission and once we receive it. Changes to Privacy Policy If we decided to change our privacy policy, we will post those changes to this privacy statement, the homepage, and other places we deem appropriate so that you are aware of what information we collect, how we use it, and under what circumstances, if any, we disclose it. We reserve the right to modify this privacy statement at any time, so please review it frequently. If we make material changes to this policy, we will notify you here, by Email, or by means of a notice on our homepage.

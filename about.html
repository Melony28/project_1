ABOUT US

Our Story

We believe that client-care is the foundation of great hairdressing. Our hairdressing is ego-free, focusing on each client's personal goals and expectations. We strive to listen, communicate and deliver results while providing an exceptional experience to every client who walks through our doors. Dedication to our clients and in-salon work is our top priority. Our hairdressing work is fresh, meticulous and technically precise, informed by our team's collective international, runway and editorial experience, ongoing in-salon training and external education sessions with leading editorial and event stylists.
